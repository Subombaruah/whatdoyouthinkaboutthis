Why Should Common People Suffer?

For your sin, should your parents suffer? If the answer is no, then why should the parents of strangers?

So why should Pakistani people suffer for a terrorist's sin? Just like Indian common people, Pakistani citizens also face the consequences of actions they never chose.

Governments take decisions, terrorists take lives, but in the end — it’s always the innocent who bleed. This article is a voice, not for one country, but for peace, for logic, and for humanity.
